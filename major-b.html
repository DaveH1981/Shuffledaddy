Suggested Listening

Here is a list of recordings I recommend for those looking to get into underground rock music. These records are listed in no particular order. Most, but not all, of these recordings were released on independent labels. Those records below that were put out on major labels tend to provide perspective, i.e. "where it all came from".

  1. Minutemen, Double Nickels on the Dime
  2. Black Flag, Nervous Breakdown
  3. Husker Du, Zen Arcade
  4. Minor Threat, The Complete Discography
  5. Sonic Youth, Daydream Nation
  6. Dinosaur Jr, You're Living All Over Me
  7. Pavement, Slanted and Enchanted
  8. The Stooges, Fun House
  9. The Velvet Underground, White Light/White Heat
  10. Sleater-Kinney, The Woods
  11. Wipers, Is This Real?
  12. The Replacements, Let it Be
  13. Meat Puppets, Meat Puppets II
  14. Bad Brains, Bad Brains
  15. Modest Mouse, The Moon and Antarctica
  16. Wire, Pink Flag
  17. Bikini Kill, Reject All American
  18. The Jesus and Mary Chain, Psychocandy
  19. Joy Division, Closer
  20. Elliott Smith, Either/Or
  21. My Bloody Valentine, Loveless
  22. X, Wild Gift
  23. Sebadoh, III
  24. Mission of Burma, Vs.
  25. Gang of Four, Entertainment!

This list is not meant to be exhaustive, but as merely a starting point. If you would like some more recommendations, feel free to Email me. A link to my Email is on the front page.
